CAD Designs


Stony Brook Motorsports Suspension Assembly

Full CAD of Vehicle Dynamics System. Includes brakes, steering, and suspension subassemblies. Every CAD change in the assembly requires a system integration meeting with other system leaders to avoid packaging problems. Optimal suspension and steering linkage lengths determined from kinematic analysis performed in LOTUS suspension software. Linkage lengths optimized for minimal toe and camber angle changes during bumps, rolls, and steering input.


Senior Design IS429 Middle School Renewable Electric Heating and Cooling System

Our senior design group was responsible for replacing existing gas boilers with a renewable electric heating system. Achieved goal of providing hot air and water via electric power. Reduced operating cost by 10%. Calculated school's monthly heating energy load, thermal resistance of structure, amount of air flow needed, and average and peak power for school year. Researched and integrated suitable heat pumps to existing piping, ductwork, and air-handling units. Created vendor parts list, estimated capital and operating cost, and lead times for deliveries. Special thanks to New York City School Construction Authority (NYCSCA) who supported and guided the scope of our project!

New electric heat pumps in blue. Old air-handling units in yellow. Solar panels in black.


Mars Rover

Sophmore year project. Mars rover with independent suspension and rocker-boogie linkage system. Camera and robotic arm attached on hood of rover to record and collect rock samples. Multi-Mission Radioisotope Thermoelectric Generator (MMRTG) equipped in back of rover to generate power and heat during missions.
