Hobbies


Hiking

The first picture on the left is Crater Lake. First time seeing a volcano in the middle of a lake which was wild. And yea, the water was really that blue. No editing needed. The second picture is Mount Rainier - Skyline Trail. Loved this trail because, unlike other hikes which make you summit a mountain first for the view, there was a pretty sight at every point in the hike. Can't go wrong with Mount Rainier. The last two pictures are from The Enchantments Trail. This 20mi hike took us roughly 24hours to complete because we decided to make a lot of stops for pictures. Can you blame us? No doubt, doing this hike in a day with vans on was a bad decision. My joints and blisters were screaming the next day.


Basketball

I grew up playing and watching basketball. I started in 3rd grade and haven't stopped. I played for my highschool team and intramurals in college. It's a fun way to do cardio and stay competitive.
